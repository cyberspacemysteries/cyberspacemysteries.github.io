Nostr Compass

Design 1

What guides our choices? Nostr is my compass is meant as a reminder to consciously bring our foundational principles into our day to day actions.

This design idea sprung from a nostr conversation with BitcoinMilkshake, who saw the possibility of creating a "compass" within the first version of the nostr.world unconference logo.


In the spirit of value for value, this design is free. If you find it meaningful or valuable, please donate, zap, or share.

Remember to give back to nostr developers and designers too.
