nostering in 5 steps

Nostr works a little different than you may be used to, but once you give it a try, it can be simple and empowering.

step 1

Choose a nostr app or website & enter it

nostr.net is keeping a good list, find options under "clients"

step 2

Get your unique set of keys

With this one set of keys you can sign-in to almost any nostr product, and it will bring up your profile information, posts, follows, and followers.

Your public key: (npub+LongStringOfNumbersUniqueToYou)

Share it with your friends so they can search for it, find you & tag you.

Your secret key: (nsec+LongStringOfNumbersUniqueToYou)

This key lets you post.

Keep it safe, no one can help you get it back if you lose it. Password managers are a good way to save them.

If in step one, you've chosen a nostr website, for security, install a browser extension to keep your keys safer.

More on keys...

step 3

Add some relays from nostr.watch's list, perhaps 8 is a magic number!?

Nostr is decentralized, which means its information is spread unto different servers across the globe. These servers running nostr software are called relays. They can be run by anyone (if you're a little tech savvy, you could run a nostr relay too). These relays do not belong to a single company, instead, the software is written so you are the one who controls your keys and your identity.

The nostr relays keep your information and share it with other relays. Adding a few relays to your nostr profile is a way to make sure your information is still available even if a relay were to go down.

step 4

Make your first post

A post saying hello world, hello nostr, or testing will do.

step 5

Find your niche

As you start following people, you will see their posts in real time on your home tab. Every once in a while visit the Global tab, a universe of people awaits there too.

For finding friends you know from Twitter, visit nostr.directory. Go ahead, add yourself to the list too.

For finding people who are most followed and most active on nostr, along with other stats, visit nostr.io/stats
