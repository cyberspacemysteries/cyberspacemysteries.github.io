    freedom weaver

    the making of a sci-fi freedom tech story

    Under the disguise of an end to conflict and an increase in an artificial ease of life, the citizens of Rigmor feel forced to accept control over their use of technology. Freedom tech is made the scapegoat, turning any innovator and human right protector into an enemy of the state.

    Unaware of the underground struggles to end this inhuman control creeping over every aspect of their lives, Toril starts digging into mysterious happenings tied to her family's past. Guided and protected by the very tech that everyone calls dangerous, it is only a matter of time before she joins those efforts, against everything society tells her to be.
