nostr-ings unwearables

unleashing designs for free
a value4value mindset


What we wear everyday can carry symbols and create memories. The designs we choose reflect part of our inner core.

Nostr has its unconferences. What would be the equivalent in wearables?

In the spirit of a value 4 value culture⤢, the designs I create will be free, both as in depicting freedom-inspired designs, and free as in you can use them to print your own wearables at your favorite local print shop.

If you find them meaningful or valuable in some way, please donate, zap, or share.
