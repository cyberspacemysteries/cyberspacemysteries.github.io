Nostr spirit

Design 0

Nostr is bigger than a person. This design hopes to honor those in nostr's & technology's past that led us here. It focuses on its key principles of freedom, decentralization, censorship-resistance & holding your own keys. It holds the hope for what the "other stuff" will bring into our lives in the future, as fellow developers, designers, and nostricans. It encourages community bonds with its shaka greetings, relays broadcasting & zaps.


In the spirit of value for value, this design is free. If you find it meaningful or valuable, please donate, zap, or share.

Remember to give back to nostr developers and designers too.
